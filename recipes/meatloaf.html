Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds of ground beef.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 chopped onion.
  • 1 cup of milk.
  • 1 cup of dried bread crumbs.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar.
  • 2 tablespoons of prepared mustard.
  • ⅓ cup of ketchup.
